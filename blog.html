About Us

  • Mission Statement

    Stocks for Charity is a byproduct of our pursuit of using investments for the community’s benefit. Our method of contributions involves using our portfolio to build up funds, which will then be used to facilitate donations to our chosen recipient, Hillsides Education Center in Pasadena.

  • AJ Wong

  • Who are we donating to?

    Funds will be directed to Hillsides. Situated between the contrastingly diverse Los Angeles areas of South Pasadena, Highland Park, and Eagle Rock, Hillsides Education Center serves the surrounding community by providing refuge and education to those who don’t have access to it. They house young individuals who aren’t fortunate enough to do so at home, work on developing their kids in their education, but most of all, are there to support and create a lasting change for America’s future. Their network of staff, contributors, and volunteers work tirelessly around the clock to give back and propel the youth to unparalleled heights. They focus solely on putting children first, and “envision a world in which children and young adults, families, and communities are able to heal, grow, and thrive”. From our connection in sharing the same ideologies and personally having experience in what Hillsides strives to do, we thought it only right to help as much as we could in upholding those values.
